# Portuguese-Bank-Marketing-Analysis
End-to-end analysis of a Portuguese bank marketing campaign using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

## Business Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze a Portuguese bankâ€™s marketing campaign data to identify key factors influencing customer subscription to term deposits and improve campaign effectiveness.

## Dataset
- Source: Portuguese Bank Marketing Dataset
- Total Customers: ~45,000
- Target Variable: Subscription (Yes/No)

## Tools Used
- SQL (SSMS): Data cleaning, KPI calculation, segmentation
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib): Exploratory data analysis
- Power BI: Interactive dashboard and business storytelling

## Key KPIs
- Total Customers
- Total Subscribers
- Conversion Rate
- Job-wise Subscription Performance
- Contact Method Effectiveness
- Previous Campaign Outcome Impact
- Campaign Fatigue Analysis

## Key Insights
- Customers with successful previous campaigns have significantly higher conversion rates
- Cellular contact performs better than telephone
- Repeated campaign exposure leads to reduced conversion (campaign fatigue)
- Certain job categories consistently show higher subscription intent

## Business Recommendations
- Prioritize customers with previous campaign success
- Focus marketing efforts on cellular contact
- Reduce repeated contacts to avoid campaign fatigue
- Customize campaigns based on job segmentation

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates end-to-end analytical thinking, from raw data analysis to actionable business recommendations.
